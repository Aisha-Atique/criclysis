PCA

There are several indicators available to measure the various contributions of prized athletes. Unfortunately, these indicators are generally highly correlated, making it difficult to judge overall player performance.
Principal Component Analysis is a technique used to emphasize variation, removes redundancy and brings out strong patterns.
It focuses not only on evaluating performances of players but also finds new players for the teams. Ranking of all players is generated with weighted-average approach.

AHP

Analytical Hierarchy Process is a Multi Criteria Decision Method which takes numerous attributes of cricket as criteria's and scale them according to preferences. Weights are assigned to every criteria, perform a consistency check so that you wont have wrong predictions.Players are ranked using Technique for Order Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution (TOPSIS) which then generates ranking of players accordingly.
