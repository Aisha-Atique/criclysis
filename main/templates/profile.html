How It Works

Create Teams

Create teams in range of 1 to 5, each team can have 16 players.
After creating teams a random order will be assigned for each team to pick a player that each team can pick on its turn.

Analyze Players

Analyze player performances by there rankings for which Data Analysis Algorithms i.e PCA and AHP have been designed.
You can also analyze each player's stats provided on their profiles.
You can filter out players of your choice.

Select Players

After satisfied with your analysis you can select any player available by visiting its profile. Remember and be careful to select player on that team turn.

List Teams

After selecting you can view teams players.

Reset Player and Teams

If not statisfied in settings you can reset all players of teams or even all teams anytime.
